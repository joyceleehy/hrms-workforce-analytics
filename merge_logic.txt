Power Query Merge Logic

Recommended approach: Append, not join.

Reason:
HRMS_A and HRMS_B contain employee records from different source systems. The goal is to stack both datasets into one standardized master workforce table.

Logic:
1. Clean HRMS_A into the target schema.
2. Clean HRMS_B into the same target schema.
3. Append HRMS_A_Clean and HRMS_B_Clean.
4. Remove duplicate employee_id values.
5. Add employee_status.
6. Load the master query to Power BI or export as cleaned_master_data.csv.

Target schema:
employee_id
employee_name
department
job_level
hire_date
termination_date
termination_type
salary
monthly_cost
budget_cost
source_system
employee_status
